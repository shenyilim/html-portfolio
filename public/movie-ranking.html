The Best Movies According to Shen Yi

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Naruto: Shippuden

Naruto Uzumaki is a spirited and determined young ninja from the Hidden Leaf Village, known as Konohagakure. Born with the powerful Nine-Tails fox spirit sealed within him, Naruto grew up facing ostracism and loneliness from the villagers who feared and scorned him. Despite this, Naruto possesses an unyielding spirit and a desire to prove himself.

Crazy Rich Asians

Rachel, a professor, dates a man named Nick and looks forward to meeting his family. However, she is shaken up when she learns that Nick belongs to one of the richest families in the country.

The intern

A seventy-year-old Ben Whittaker realises that retirement isn't an enjoyable experience. As a result, he decides to work as an intern at an online fashion store managed by an extremely sceptical boss.
